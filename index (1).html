About Me

Dual-Track Professional. Single-Minded Ambition.

I'm Yashesh Trivedi — a US IT Recruiter at Alois Technologies and an Industrial Trading Professional at Radhey Trading Company (RTC), based in Vadodara, India. My career is built at the intersection of technology recruitment and industrial commerce.

In recruitment, I specialize in sourcing and screening candidates for W2, C2C, and 1099 engagements across the US market. I work closely with clients and hiring managers to deliver quality-matched talent — using Boolean search, LinkedIn Recruiter, and deep candidate engagement to close positions efficiently.

On the industrial side, I support the growth of Radhey Trading Company, which deals in welding consumables, grinding and cutting discs, and power tools. My focus is on dealer network development, B2B outreach, and expanding market presence across Gujarat and beyond.

My vision is to grow both verticals into thriving, scalable businesses — leveraging cross-domain thinking and an entrepreneurial mindset.
